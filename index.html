Question: What's all this... for exactly?
To preserve knowledge and make it easily accessible for... those that will come after.
Question: What motivated you to make this site?
The Horizon Franchise is one of my all time favourites, because you can actually feel the love and effort put into making it through the in-game lore.
I'd go so far as to say: narrative is one of the prime gameplay elements. Without the incredible story, this franchise would not be half as beloved as it is.
If this archive can remind even a single person why they love this series, I'll consider myself successful.
Question: How did you extract all this data?
Most of the text can be found online. As for any audio you may encounter, it had to be manually (tediously) extracted through screen recordings.
